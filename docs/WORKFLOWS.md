## ðŸ” Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Score Not Improving?
1. Check utilization is actually under 10%
2. Verify all accounts are added
3. Ensure payments are on time
4. Look for errors on credit reports
5. Consider credit builder loans

### Utilization Still High?
1. Pay down highest-balance cards first
2. Request credit limit increases
3. Avoid new charges before statement dates
4. Pay twice monthly (before and after statement)
5. Consider balance transfer options

### Missing Reminders?
1. Check automation is enabled
2. Verify email/SMS settings
3. Update notification preferences
4. Check spam/junk folders
5. Whitelist alerts@credstack.com

### Disputes Not Resolving?
1. Ensure all documentation is complete
2. Follow up at 30-day mark
3. Escalate to CFPB if needed
4. Consider professional help for complex issues
5. Keep detailed records
